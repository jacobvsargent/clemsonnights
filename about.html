About

CLEMSON NIGHTS offers your solution to "where are we going tonight" for a night out in Clemson. No clutter, just real deals.

CLEMSON NIGHTS is a collection of weekly specials, deals, and events for the restaurants/bars in and around Clemson, South Carolina. A project by Emma and Jacob Sargent, CN seeks to make it easier for Clemson students to understand where the best deals are without having to navigate a ton of different business's Facebook and Instagram pages.

Special thanks to Jesse Dennis for her list of Clemson specials & deals!
